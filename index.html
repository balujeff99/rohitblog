Rohit Sharma

Rohit Gurunath Sharma (born 30 April 1987) is an Indian international cricketer and captain of the Indian cricket team in all formats. He plays as a right-handed opening batter for the Indian national cricket team and for the IPL-side Mumbai Indians. Considered as one of the best white-ball openers of his era, Sharma is known for his timing, six-hitting ability and leadership, and holds record for scoring most hundreds (five) in a single Cricket World Cup. Sharma captains Mumbai Indians in the Indian Premier League (IPL) and plays for Mumbai in domestic cricket. In the IPL, the Mumbai Indians have won the tournament a record five times under his leadership.
